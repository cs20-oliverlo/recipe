Sriracha Scramble Eggs

Description

My favourite scrambled egg recipe Sriracha Scrambled Eggs. Make this with normal sized eggs and definitely unsalted butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Sriracha
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat a skillet to low-medium heat (low if you like softer eggs.
  2. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl.
  3. Beat eggs until single mixture.
  4. Mix in a pinch of salt and some pepper until thoroughly combined.
  5. Add Sriracha and mix until there are no clumps left.
  6. Add unsalted butter to the skillet and wait until fully melted.
  7. Add egg mixture to pan and continuously fold until cooked to your liking.
  8. Enjoy!
